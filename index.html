Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Nocturne Lead Emails Extractor is designed to extract publicly visible email addresses from the webpage that the user chooses to scan.

Information We Process

  • Publicly visible email addresses found on the active webpage.
  • The user's Gumroad license key (stored locally only for PRO activation).

How Data Is Used

The extension only processes the currently active webpage when the user clicks the Extract button.

No extracted email addresses are transmitted to our servers.

The extension does not collect passwords, payment information, browsing history, personal messages, location, or any sensitive information.

Storage

Your PRO license key is stored locally using Chrome Storage in order to remember your activation status.

Third Parties

The extension communicates with Gumroad only to verify the validity of your license.

No other third-party services receive your extracted data.

Your Data

All extracted information remains on your device unless you explicitly export it as a CSV file.
